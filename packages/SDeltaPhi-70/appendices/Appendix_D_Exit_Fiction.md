# Appendix D — Exit Fiction: Afterlife, Liberation, AI Utopia, and AI Dystopia

This appendix is an illustrative application, not a comparative religion or political theology replacement.

## Function in SΔϕ-70

Exit Fiction names fictional structures whose solution is not simply stronger habitation within the present world, but escape, transcendence, liberation, relocation, replacement, or termination of the present-world cost structure.

## Examples

- Afterlife doctrines may relocate habitation beyond the present world.
- Liberation doctrines may seek exit from attachment, suffering, or cyclic binding.
- AI utopia narratives may imagine technological transcendence of current human limits.
- AI dystopia narratives may imagine collapse or replacement of present-world human structures.

## SΔϕ classification

- Fiction type: Exit Fiction
- Binding Fiction Score: varies by tradition or system
- Editing Activity: varies
- Exit Fiction Gate: ON or PARTIAL

## Key sentence

Exit Fiction is not a low Binding Fiction score. It is a different direction of fictional solution.
