# Appendix E — Closed Fiction: Private Thoughts That Fail to Bind

This appendix is an illustrative application, not a dismissal of private thought.

## Function in SΔϕ-70

Closed Fiction is the most common and ordinary form of unbound internal Δϕ: thoughts, plans, images, worlds, theories, or desires that never leave the internal field or leave it without durable World-Binding.

## Interpretation

Private thoughts may be intense. They may produce real internal Δϕ. But if they do not bind to record, relation, action, body, institution, repeated practice, or shared world, they remain volatile.

This does not make them worthless. A closed fiction may be protected, private, early-stage, incubating, or deliberately non-public. The diagnostic question is not whether it has value, but whether it has become world-bound.

## SΔϕ classification

- Fiction type: Closed / Volatile Fiction
- Binding Fiction Score: 1
- Editing Activity: 0-1
- Exit Fiction Gate: usually OFF

## Key sentence

The lowest Binding Fiction is not the false story, but the unbound thought that never gains a path into the world.
