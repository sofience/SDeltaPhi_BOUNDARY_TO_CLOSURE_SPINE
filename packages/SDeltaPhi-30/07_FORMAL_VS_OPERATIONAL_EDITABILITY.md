# Formal vs Operational Editability

```text
formal editability
≠
operational editability
```

Formal editability exists when a clause, button, promise, consultation, appeal, or revision procedure is present.

Operational editability exists when the default path is actually revised, rollback is timely, exceptions re-enter, and multiple actors can materially alter the path.

SΔϕ-30 audits the gap between these two states.
