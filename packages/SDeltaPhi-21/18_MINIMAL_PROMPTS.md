# Minimal Prompts

## Prompt 1 — Judgment

Apply SΔϕ-21.
Classify judgment as authority assignment over intuition markers, not truth.

## Prompt 2 — Authority Assignment

Apply SΔϕ-21.
Map I to A(I) and determine the executable route.

## Prompt 3 — Authority Debt

Apply SΔϕ-21.
Check whether misassigned authority has increased revision resistance over time.

## Prompt 4 — Diagnostic Regime

Apply SΔϕ-21.
Classify the regime as under-authority/drift, over-authority/closure, or editable judgment/open persistence.

## Prompt 5 — Editability Conditions

Apply SΔϕ-21.
Check conflict intake, marker separation, revision budget, rollback handle, and maintenance mode.

## Prompt 6 — Governance Bridge

Apply SΔϕ-21.
Ask who or what controls authority revision triggers.

## Prompt 7 — Anti-overclaim

Apply SΔϕ-21 and SΔϕ-62.
Do not infer truth, rationality, legal authority, moral authority, or intuition reliability from authority assignment alone.
