## Role

SΔϕ-21 is the **judgment / authority assignment / editable decision layer** of the Sofience–Δϕ Formalism.

It defines judgment not as truth, psychology, rationality, moral certainty, or legal authority, but as the operation that assigns authority to intuition markers.

## Core compression

```text
Judgment := J: I → A(I).

I = intuition marker.
A = authority, priority weight, or gating rule.
J = authority assignment over intuition markers.

Closure occurs when A(I) becomes non-editable.

Drift occurs when A(I) fails to stabilize.

Editable judgment occurs when A(I) is stable but revisable at finite cost.
```
