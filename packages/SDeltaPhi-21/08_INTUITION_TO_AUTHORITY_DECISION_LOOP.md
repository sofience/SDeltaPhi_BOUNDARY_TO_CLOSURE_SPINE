# Intuition → Authority → Decision Loop

SΔϕ-21 models judgment as an authority gate layered on top of intuition.

```text
Intuition markers
I₁ … Iₙ
        ↓
Authority assignment
A(I)
        ↓
Decision / action
route r*
        ↓
Authority debt Dᴀ
revision resistance
        ↺
Re-entry
next state biased by r*
```

## Function

Authority assignment produces an executable decision route.

The executed route re-enters the system and biases future authority assignments.

## Risk

Without editability, authority debt accumulates and judgment becomes self-reinforcing.
