# Judgment to Governance Bridge

SΔϕ-21 shifts the governance question.

If judgment is authority assignment over intuition markers, then the central governance question becomes:

```text
Who or what controls authority revision triggers?
```

## Governance shift

Not:

```text
Who has the correct judgment?
```

But:

```text
Can authority assignments be revised at finite cost?
Who controls revision triggers?
What perturbations are allowed to challenge A(I)?
```

## Bridge

```text
judgment
→ authority assignment
→ revision triggers
→ governance
```

## Boundary

This is not legal authority theory.

This is not political sovereignty theory.

It is a structural bridge from judgment to authority revision governance.
