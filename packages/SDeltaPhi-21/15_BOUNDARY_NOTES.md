# Boundary Notes

SΔϕ-21 excludes the following from its scope:

- psychology of decision-making;
- legal authority theory;
- moral authority theory;
- proof of truth;
- proof of rationality;
- proof that intuition is reliable;
- justification for non-editable authority;
- clinical or cognitive diagnosis.

This package fixes only minimal authority-assignment dynamics after no-exit intuition.

## Negative constraints

Do not treat judgment as truth.

Do not treat authority as legitimacy.

Do not treat intuition as automatically authoritative.

Do not treat editability as an attitude.

Classify authority assignment and finite-cost revision dynamics.
