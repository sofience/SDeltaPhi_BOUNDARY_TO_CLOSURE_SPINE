# Conflict Intake and Revision Budget

Editable judgment requires two conditions:

```text
conflict intake
+
revision budget
```

## Conflict intake

The system must allow perturbations that challenge A(I).

Without conflict intake, authority becomes insulated.

## Revision budget

The system must have enough update budget to revise A(I).

```text
UpdateBudget ≥ RevisionCost(A(I))
```

## Failure

```text
conflict blocked
OR revision cost exceeds update budget
→ A(I) becomes non-editable
→ closure risk
```
