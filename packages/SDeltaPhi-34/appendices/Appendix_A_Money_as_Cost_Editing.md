# Appendix A — Money as Cost Editing

Money is not merely exchange. It is generalized leverage over path cost.

A path may be possible in principle but inaccessible in practice because delay, risk, exhaustion, uncertainty, or repair cost is too high. Money edits this profile. It can shorten delay, buffer failure, buy expertise, purchase time, and make otherwise inaccessible continuations selectable.

Illustrative statement: money does not abolish constraint; it redistributes constraint unevenly.
