# Appendix B — Money as Rollback Reality

Rollback may formally exist while materially failing.

A person, firm, or institution may be told that return, exit, repair, or retry is available. But if the cost of return is too high, rollback is only nominal. Money strengthens rollback reality by lowering the cost of return, retry, exit, survival, and repair.

Illustrative statement: the same error may be terminal for one actor and recoverable for another.
