## Core Claim

Money edits path cost. Capital reproduces path advantage.

In SΔϕ-34, money is not treated merely as medium of exchange, unit of account, or store of value. Money is interpreted as **generalized cost editing**: a capacity to lower friction, shorten delay, buffer failure, widen maneuverability, strengthen rollback reality, and sustain exception persistence.

Capital is not treated as merely accumulated money. Capital is interpreted as **default reproduction**: a structure that repeatedly stabilizes favorable continuations, normalizes selected paths, raises rollback and re-entry costs for others, and redistributes authority over default editing.

## AI Use

Use this package to diagnose economic power as path power:

- What path costs are lowered?
- Whose rollback reality is strengthened?
- Which non-default paths can persist?
- Which defaults are reproduced?
- Whose rollback or re-entry costs are raised?
- Who gains the authority to define the field of choice?
