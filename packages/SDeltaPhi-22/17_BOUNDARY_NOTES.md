# Boundary Notes

SΔϕ-22 excludes the following from its scope:

- legal procedure;
- political doctrine;
- moral authority theory;
- institutional design by itself;
- psychological therapy;
- proof that all authority should be weakened;
- justification for endless revision;
- clinical or cognitive diagnosis.

This package fixes only minimal authority-editing dynamics after judgment.

## Negative constraints

Do not equate open persistence with belief in openness.

Do not treat conflict as automatically harmful.

Do not treat authority as automatically bad.

Do not treat revision as endless revision.

Classify trigger handling, finite-cost editability, rollback handles, authority debt, and re-entry governance.
