# Marker-Authority Isolation

AEP requires separating intuition marker I from authority A.

```text
I ≠ A
```

## Failure

```text
I ≡ A
→ identity collapse
→ authority becomes self-justifying
→ closure risk
```

## Function

The system may preserve the intuition marker while editing its assigned authority.

## Rule

Do not delete I merely because A was misassigned.

Do not preserve A merely because I remains strong.
