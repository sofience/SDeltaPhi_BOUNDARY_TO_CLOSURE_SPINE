# Case Pattern A — Educational Cost Theater

## Case type

Reported classroom comparison / educational demonstration case.

## Surface interpretation

```text
free AI vs paid AI performance comparison
```

## SΔϕ-68 interpretation

```text
visible price vs transition completion cost comparison
```

## Pattern

A free-tier system appears more accessible because direct payment cost is lower.

However, the student may bear hidden costs:

```text
repeated prompting
source checking
weaker context continuity
output repair
citation verification
time loss
quality uncertainty
```

A paid-tier system may impose higher direct cost while lowering task-completion friction.

## Boundary

Do not use this pattern to claim that free AI is useless or paid AI is morally superior.

Use it to show that access price and task-completion burden are separate fields.
