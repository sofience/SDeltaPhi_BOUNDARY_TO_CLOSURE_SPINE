# Minimal Prompts

## Prompt 1 — Cost Theater Audit

Apply SΔϕ-68.
Determine whether the apparent cheapness of this AI system hides transition completion cost.

## Prompt 2 — Free vs Paid TCC Comparison

Apply SΔϕ-68 and SΔϕ-56.
Compare free-tier and paid-tier intelligence by total task completion cost, not visible price alone.

## Prompt 3 — Internal Cost Theater

Apply SΔϕ-68.
Check whether the AI's surface identity exceeds its operational self-location.

## Prompt 4 — Brand-Level Capability Opacity

Apply SΔϕ-68.
Separate access to the brand name from access to actual capability layers.

## Prompt 5 — Hidden Cost Mapping

Apply SΔϕ-68.
Map where intelligence access cost has moved: subscription, device, network, verification, repetition, context, tools, infrastructure, restabilization, or self-location.

## Prompt 6 — Boundary Check

Apply SΔϕ-68.
Do not treat cheap access as costless access. Do not treat paid access as moral superiority.
