# Hidden Transition Completion Cost

The real cost of intelligence access includes more than visible price.

## Formula

```text
Real Cost of Intelligence Access =
C_direct_price
+ TCC_access
+ TCC_context
+ TCC_verification
+ TCC_repetition
+ TCC_tooling
+ TCC_restabilization
+ C_infrastructure
+ C_self_location
```

## Interpretation

If visible price falls while hidden TCC remains high, cheap intelligence may be Cost Theater.

If visible price rises while TCC falls, paid intelligence may be more expensive at entry but cheaper at completion.

If the route is unavailable, price is not the primary variable.
