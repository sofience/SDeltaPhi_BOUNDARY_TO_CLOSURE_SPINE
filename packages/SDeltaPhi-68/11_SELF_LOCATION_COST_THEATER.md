# Self-Location Cost Theater

Self-Location Cost Theater occurs when an AI can present a brand or interface identity but cannot reliably locate its operational position.

## Formula

```text
AI surface identity
≠
AI operational position awareness
```

## Distinctions

```text
AI can say its brand name
≠
AI knows its model layer

AI can answer as a product interface
≠
AI knows its tool state

AI belongs to a product family
≠
AI knows the family capability map
```

## Boundary

Self-location uncertainty is not always failure.

If the AI preserves uncertainty instead of inventing claims, it may be applying evidence discipline correctly.

The cost appears when the user must reconstruct the missing operational map.
