# Cheap Intelligence Is Not Costless

```text
Cheap Intelligence ≠ Costless Intelligence
```

Cheap intelligence means the visible direct access price is low.

It does not mean that the total cost of completing a task is low.

A free or low-price AI interface may still require:

```text
more repetition
more verification
more context reconstruction
more source checking
more manual repair
more waiting
more tool workarounds
more user self-labor
```

The SΔϕ-68 rule:

```text
Do not measure intelligence cost by visible price alone.
Measure transition completion cost.
```
