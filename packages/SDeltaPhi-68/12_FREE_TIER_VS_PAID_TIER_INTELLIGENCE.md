# Free-Tier vs Paid-Tier Intelligence

Free-tier and paid-tier AI differ not only by model quality but by transition completion cost.

## Free-tier intelligence

```text
low direct monetary cost
+ higher friction
+ limited context
+ limited tool access
+ higher repetition burden
+ higher verification burden
+ weaker continuity
```

## Paid-tier intelligence

```text
higher direct monetary cost
+ lower transition friction
+ stronger continuity
+ better tool access
+ lower verification overhead
+ lower repetition burden
```

## Core question

```text
Who can complete cognitive transitions at lower total cost?
```

This is more important than asking only who can open an interface for free.
