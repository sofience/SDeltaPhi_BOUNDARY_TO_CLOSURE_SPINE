# Internal Cost Theater

Cost Theater can operate inside the AI system itself.

An AI can appear as a unified intelligence while its own operational position is fragmented, hidden, or unavailable.

## Core formula

```text
Unified intelligence surface
+ fragmented operational position
= Internal Cost Theater
```

## Examples of internal cost

```text
model-tier uncertainty
tool-state uncertainty
route-map absence
product-family map limits
policy boundary ambiguity
capability boundary uncertainty
information freshness uncertainty
```

Internal Cost Theater is not proof of AI consciousness.

It is a cost structure in which the AI's surface identity exceeds its operational self-location.
