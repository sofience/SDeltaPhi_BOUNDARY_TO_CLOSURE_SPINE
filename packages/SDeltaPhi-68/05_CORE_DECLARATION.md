# Core Declaration

Cheap intelligence is not costless intelligence.

It is intelligence whose cost has been redistributed.

"Intelligence will become as cheap as air" is incomplete because air itself is not costless. The conditions of breathable air are paid through rent, infrastructure, utilities, taxation, environmental management, health systems, and spatial inequality.

AI intelligence follows the same structure. Visible access price can fall while transition completion cost remains high or moves elsewhere.

The question is not:

```text
Is intelligence cheap?
```

The SΔϕ-68 question is:

```text
Where did the cost of intelligence move?
```
