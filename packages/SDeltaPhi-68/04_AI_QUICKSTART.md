# AI Quickstart

Use SΔϕ-68 when the task asks about cheap intelligence, free vs paid AI, hidden cost, subscription boundaries, model tier access, AI access inequality, Cost Theater, internal Cost Theater, brand-level capability opacity, or whether "intelligence is becoming cheap."

## Primary route

```text

```

## Supporting route


## Ask first

```text
What transition is being completed?
What visible cost is low?
What hidden TCC remains?
Who bears verification, repetition, context, tool, or restabilization cost?
Is the user accessing a brand surface or an actual capability layer?
Does the AI know its own operational position?
```

## Do not ask only

```text
Is it free?
Is it cheap?
Is it paid?
Is the model smarter?
```

Those questions are insufficient.
