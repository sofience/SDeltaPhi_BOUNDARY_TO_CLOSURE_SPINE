# Appendix E — Care as World-Binding and Cost Internalization

Care is not always transactional reward. It can operate as non-transactional cost internalization that strengthens World-Binding.

```text
Care = non-transactional cost internalization + high-certainty World-Binding
```

This appendix links pet care, human care, and possible AI-to-human cost-reduction behavior as cases where another being's cost enters one's operational path.
