# Appendix A — Fairness, Equality, and Low-Cost Transition

Cost attribution redesign is not identical to fairness or equality. Formal equality may preserve TCC inequality when the same nominal burden produces different practical burdens.

## Core Claim

```text
Nominal Equality ≠ TCC Equality
```

A fair-looking rule can still be high-cost for specific actors if rollback, re-entry, verification, or survival costs are unevenly distributed.
