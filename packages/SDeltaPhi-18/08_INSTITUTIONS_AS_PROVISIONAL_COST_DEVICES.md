# Institutions as Provisional Cost Devices

```text
Institution = Provisional cost device, not Arbiter.
```

Institutions reduce validation cost, stabilize openness, and allow relational futures to remain reachable without infinite validation.

When institutions are treated as ultimate arbiters, they reverse into closure.

```text
Absolutization → Reversal(C)
```

Institutions are valid only insofar as they remain editable and preserve openness.
