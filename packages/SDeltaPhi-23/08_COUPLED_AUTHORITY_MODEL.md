# Coupled Authority Model

In co-environmentalization, authority assignment and revision are no longer local decisions.

```text
A_x<->y
```

## Model

```text
I_x → A_x → action ↔ re-entry → I_y → A_y → action
tau_x ↔ propagation ↔ tau_y
AEP_x ↔ governance ↔ AEP_y
```

## Meaning

Authority edits in X affect Y's next transition.

Authority edits in Y affect X's next transition.

Authority becomes a shared constraint field rather than a private preference.

## Risk

One-sided closure can propagate into coupled closure.
