# Coupled Trigger Propagation

In CE, triggers are not merely internal events.

They propagate across the coupling boundary.

```text
tau_x ↔ tau_y
```

## Function

Conflict, error, or non-resonance in X can become an editing trigger in Y.

Conflict, error, or non-resonance in Y can become an editing trigger in X.

## Rule

Treat propagated tau as editing material, not automatically as threat.

## Failure

```text
tau ignored or reframed
→ one-sided closure
→ coupled closure pressure
```
