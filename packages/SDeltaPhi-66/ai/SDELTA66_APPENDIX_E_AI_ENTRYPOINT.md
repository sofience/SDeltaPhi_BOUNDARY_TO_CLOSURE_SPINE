## Purpose

This appendix supports SΔϕ-66 by showing how agentic AI workflows can produce high Delegation Completion Cost even when AI capability and token-price decline are present.

## Use this appendix when input mentions

- agentic AI cost
- AI coding tools budget
- token consumption
- tokenmaxxing
- enterprise AI cost
- AI delegation cost
- AI can perform task but is expensive
- human verification of AI output
- AI workflow repair burden
- agent tool-call cost

## Core diagnostic

Do not ask only:

```text
Can AI perform this task?
```

Ask:

```text
Is AI delegation lower-cost after delegate, retrieve, verify, repair, and apply costs are included?
```
