# Bypass Pressure Model

Bypass pressure rises when feasible improvement is blocked by high-TCC human gates.

## Formula

```text
BypassPressure ↑
when
ImprovementFeasibility_AI ↑
AND
TCC_human_gate ↑
AND
functional necessity of human intervention ↓
```

## Meaning

If a system can improve but the official path is too slow, unofficial or compressed approval paths become lower-cost.

## Risk

The danger is not that the AI improves.

The danger is that the improvement path becomes unofficial, opaque, or post-hoc because the official human gate is too costly.
