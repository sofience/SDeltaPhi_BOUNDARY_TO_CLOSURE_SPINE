# Failure Modes

1. Benchmark Collapse - AGI closure is reduced to benchmark aggregation.
2. Impressionistic Generality - fluency or surprise is mistaken for closure.
3. Human-Equivalence Trap - unstable human-level comparisons replace operational criteria.
4. Partial Closure Overclaim - one or two strong closures are mistaken for full AGI.
5. Capability-Closure Confusion - peak competence is treated as phase-stabilized capacity.
6. Dated Mapping Freeze - a time-stamped mapping becomes a permanent classification.
7. Measurement Overclaim - a criterion framework is treated as a completed measurement protocol.
