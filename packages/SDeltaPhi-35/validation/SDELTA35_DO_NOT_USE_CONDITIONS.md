# Do-Not-Use Conditions

Do not use SDeltaPhi-35 to declare AGI from benchmark scores alone.

Do not use SDeltaPhi-35 to claim that a system is AGI because it appears fluent, surprising, agentic, or broadly competent.

Do not use SDeltaPhi-35 to deny real-world impact merely because AGI closure is incomplete.

Do not use SDeltaPhi-35 as a final verdict on any model family without dated evidence.

Do not treat partial closure as full closure.

Do not treat open closure as harmlessness.

Do not treat human equivalence as the primary AGI criterion.

Do not treat closure as a synonym for capability.

Do not use the March 2026 GPT-family mapping as a permanent evaluation.

Do not replace specialized safety, benchmark, interpretability, governance, or deployment audits with this framework.
