# Pre-detection and Irreversibility

Pre-detection does not replace irreversibility.

It clarifies what lies immediately before irreversibility becomes historically legible.

## Sequence

```text
latent operative difference
-> threshold crossing
-> event registration
-> irreversible consequence
```

## Rule

Irreversibility does not begin at mere variation.

It begins when variation becomes structurally consequential through registration and re-entry.

## Boundary

Pre-detection is not a rival ontology to irreversibility.

It is the minimal precursor layer before irreversible registration.
