# Authority as Detection Bandwidth

SΔϕ-25 extends the authority sequence by defining authority as a condition on detectability.

Authority does not merely judge events after they appear.

Authority conditions what may appear as an event in the first place.

```text
authority -> detection bandwidth
```

## Authority determines whether a difference is

```text
detectable
ignorable
suppressible
unspeakable
```

## Implication

Authority shapes reality indirectly by shaping the field of event registration.

## Boundary

Authority does not create all differences.

Authority governs which differences can enter recognized eventhood.
