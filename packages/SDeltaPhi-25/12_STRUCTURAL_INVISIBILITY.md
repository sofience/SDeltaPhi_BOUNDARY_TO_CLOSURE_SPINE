# Structural Invisibility

Structural invisibility is active, not empty.

The undetected is not necessarily inert.

It may already be shaping the next state.

## Examples of structural transition before naming

```text
political shift before official recognition
social instability before visible crisis
conceptual innovation before vocabulary
personal transformation before conscious interpretation
```

## Core rule

```text
detectable event != beginning of change
```

The detectable event may be the first successful registration of a longer latent process.
