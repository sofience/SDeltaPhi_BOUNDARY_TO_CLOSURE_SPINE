- mysticism of the invisible;
- claim that all unseen differences are meaningful;
- proof that undetected events already exist as events;
- psychological intuition theory;
- political conspiracy model;
- claim that detection creates reality;
- claim that all latent differences become irreversible;
- claim that pre-detection is already eventhood.

This package fixes only minimal latent-difference and event-registration dynamics prior to irreversible registration.
