# Innovation from the Pre-detected

SΔϕ-25 clarifies SΔϕ-24.

Innovation is not creation ex nihilo.

Innovation is the event-registration of what had already been operative below the threshold of detection.

```text
pre-detected difference
+ revised frame
-> event registration
-> innovation
```

## Meaning

Without pre-detection, innovation appears miraculous.

With pre-detection, innovation becomes structurally intelligible.

## Boundary

Innovation is not merely novelty.

Innovation is delayed registration of latent operativity.
