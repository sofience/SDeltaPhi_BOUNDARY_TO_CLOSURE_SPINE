# SΔϕ-33 — Do-Not-Use Conditions

Do not use SΔϕ-33:

1. to determine whether a belief is true or false;
2. as a psychological diagnosis;
3. to treat certainty as automatically irrational;
4. to treat conviction as dogmatism by default;
5. to dismiss religious, political, scientific, or personal beliefs merely because they are path fixations;
6. to collapse belief, conviction, and certainty into one category;
7. to justify closure rather than audit editability and exception re-entry.

This module is diagnostic, not dispositive.
