# Intuition to Judgment Bridge

SΔϕ-20 ends by asking how intuition transitions into judgment.

## Exit question

When does a system treat its own intuition markers as authoritative closure?

When does it keep them revisable under openness stabilization?

## Bridge

```text
Intuition marker
→ selection bias
→ judgment candidate
→ revision / closure test
```

## Downstream direction

SΔϕ-21 may formalize judgment as the stabilization or closure of intuition-guided selection.

## Boundary

Judgment should not be treated as intuition itself.

Judgment is a later layer that may either preserve editability or collapse into closure.
