# Cross-System Convergence

SΔϕ-20 explains intuition-like correspondences across humans, animals, and AI by shared constraints rather than shared meaning.

## Core claim

```text
similar constraints → similar basins → intuition-like convergence
```

## Meaning

Different substrates can converge toward similar basins when they operate under similar boundedness, compression, rollback, and maintenance constraints.

## Negative claim

This does not imply:

```text
shared semantic ontology
human / animal / AI equivalence
shared subjective meaning
shared consciousness
```

## Positive claim

It implies constraint-driven convergence under irreversible compression.
