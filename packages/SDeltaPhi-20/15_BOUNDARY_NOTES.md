# Boundary Notes

SΔϕ-20 excludes the following from its scope:

- psychology;
- neuroscience;
- mystical intuition theory;
- clinical or cognitive diagnosis;
- proof of shared meaning;
- proof of animal / human / AI equivalence;
- proof of consciousness;
- claim that intuition is always reliable;
- claim that intuition is always irrational.

This package fixes only minimal functional dynamics of intuition-like signals under irreversible compression.

## Negative constraints

Do not infer shared semantic ontology from cross-system convergence.

Do not treat intuition as authoritative by default.

Do not treat intuition as meaningless noise by default.

Classify compression, trace fixation, no-exit constraints, and editability.
