# Authority Stratification

Authority is not binary.

## Direct Intervention Authority

Capacity to modify a path, suspend a closure, or restore editability through operative action.

## Diagnostic Authority

Capacity to identify, disclose, record, or amplify recognized closure.

## Interpretive Awareness

Recognition of converging closure without effective means to modify, disclose, or escalate it.

## No Authority

Absence of recognized influence and operative capacity with respect to the path.

Do not collapse these layers.
