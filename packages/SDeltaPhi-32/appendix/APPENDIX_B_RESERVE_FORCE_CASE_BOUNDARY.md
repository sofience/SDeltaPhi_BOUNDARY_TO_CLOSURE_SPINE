# Appendix B — Reserve-Force Case Boundary

The reserve-force case pattern must not be used as:

```text
a legal finding
a final causation judgment
a claim of institutional fault
a claim that reserve-force intensification is always illegitimate
a claim that reserve-force training is unnecessary
a claim that all military training incidents prove replacement closure
```

The case is used only as a structural audit trigger.

The SΔϕ-32 question is:

```text
Did the intervention preserve rollback, exception re-entry, editability, and no replacement closure?
```
